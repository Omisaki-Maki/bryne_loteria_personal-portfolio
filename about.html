Inspirations

Games that shaped how I think, create, and push forward.

Minecraft

Minecraft has been the biggest inspiration for me in terms of me getting into software development. Minecraft is a game that made me creative and made me realize that building is fun. But more than that. The command blocks in Minecraft introduced me to programming, wherein inputting instructions will produce different results that can make anything in the game possible.

Hollow Knight

My next inspiration is Hollow Knight, this game is an inspiration because even a small developer team can make a simple game like this into something popular, and are open enough to let the community of players to modify and alter the game. Which introduced me into scripting, and game modding.

Hollow Knight: Silksong

The sequel to the first hollow knight game, Silksong is the next game that allowed me to apply my programming skills into creating my own simple mods. This game has been a quick realization of how far I've become in terms of software development.

Genshin Impact

Although and Open World RPG, Genshin's combat system and calculations are very complex. I am a player that enjoys combat and strategies and seeing that math and formulas apply into the damages I will deal in game, inspired me to attempt to recreate the combat logic in my own "Turn Based Game".
